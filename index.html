Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What if I do not have a Guitar ?

Our Teacher will help you purchase the Guitar, you can fill the Free Trial form to get in touch with the Teacher Directly and we will be happy to Help.

2. What will the days and Timings for classes ?

The teachers are available all weekdays from morning 8am to evening 8pm.

3. What will be covered in this Courses?

Our Courses will cover all important aspect of Playing Guitar.(i.e. Chords, Leads, Plucking, Music Theory , improvisation and much more). The Student will be Able to Sing along and play any song of their choice on both Leads and Chords.

4. What will happen after enrolling the course?

We will ping you on Whatsapp in the next 24 hours and then you can schedule your class with the Teacher.

5. I am Skeptical about learning online.

Nothing can overshadow the Results that we are able to deliver in the Past Years, Hence we Request you to visit and Check our student video and in addition take a free trial session.
